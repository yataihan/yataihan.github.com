   Founded in 2000, Beijing AsiaHines Decoration & Design Co., Ltd has specialized in interior design and art display for over ten years, providing professional interior furnishings design and consulting services for hotels, clubs, top villas, real estate model rooms and commercial space. Adhering to our tenet of innovation and integrity and relying on our excellent design team, AsiaHines has designed works with diverse styles targeted at different projects through accurate analysis and positioning.

   We pay great attention to continuously gathering the newest consulting cases and conduct surveys across the world to understand and adjust to the changing market. We are sensitive to the trend and can precisely define the styles of hotel groups or customer groups. Certainly we are concerned about customers’ return on investment and committed to producing superb brand value for our clients. From the interior room to the exterior space, from home design to art display design,

   AsiaHines will always focus on offering all-around design services with a good taste and producing an exclusive space for customers in a considerate way.
